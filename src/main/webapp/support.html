We are here to help you with payments, tickets, and account questions.

Frequently Asked Questions
How can I change my travel date?
You can request a date change from the “My Bookings” page by selecting the trip and choosing “Reschedule”.
What happens if my train is canceled?
If your train is canceled, you will receive a full refund or the option to book another trip without extra fees.
Can I get a refund after payment?
Refund rules depend on the ticket type and time before departure. Please review the refund policy or contact support.
